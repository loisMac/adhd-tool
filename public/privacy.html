Privacy & Data

Last updated: 28 March 2026

Calm Space is built and run by one person. It's designed to feel safe, low-pressure, and private. You don't need an account to use it, and what you type stays on your own device.

What Calm Space stores

Calm Space stores your tool entries in your browser using local storage. This includes things like tasks, notes, timers, and invoices.

Where your data goes

  • Your entries stay on your device in this browser.
  • Calm Space does not require an account to use the toolkit.
  • Calm Space does not sync your entries across devices.

What Calm Space does not do

  • It does not sell your task or note data.
  • It does not share your entries with other users by default.
  • It does not require sign-up before you can use core tools.

When data can be lost

  • If you clear browser/site data.
  • If you use private/incognito mode.
  • If you switch to a different browser or device.
